Animal Chess

Minimal strategy board game

Animal Chess Rules

Learn the board, pieces, terrain, captures and winning conditions for Animal Chess.

Goal

Enter the opponent den or capture all opponent pieces. Reaching the den is often faster than capturing every piece.

Piece Rank

1Rat
2Cat
3Dog
4Wolf
5Leopard
6Tiger
7Lion
8Elephant

A piece normally captures an enemy piece with equal or lower rank. Rat can capture Elephant, but Elephant cannot capture Rat.

Movement

Most animals move one square horizontally or vertically. They cannot move diagonally and cannot move onto a friendly piece.

Capture

Higher-ranked animals can capture lower-ranked animals. Equal rank can capture equal rank. Traps reduce enemy rank to 0.

River

Only Rat can enter river squares. Lion and Tiger can jump across river lanes, but a Rat in the river blocks the jump.

Trap & Den

You cannot enter your own den. Enter the opponent den to win. Traps weaken enemy pieces standing on them.

Beginner Tips

  • Do not expose Elephant to the opponent Rat.
  • Use Lion and Tiger jumps to attack quickly.
  • Control traps near the den.
  • Sometimes entering the den is faster than capturing all pieces.
